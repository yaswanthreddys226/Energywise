Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please use our live chat or phone support.

Can I request a demo of Energy Wise?

Yes! You can request a personalized demo by selecting "General Inquiry" in the subject dropdown and mentioning "Demo Request" in your message.

Do you offer support in languages other than English?

We currently offer support in English, Spanish, French, and German. Please indicate your preferred language in your message.
