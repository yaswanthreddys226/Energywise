How to Map Your MCB

  1. Turn off one MCB switch at a time
  2. Note which appliances or rooms lose power
  3. Drag the switch to the corresponding room or appliance
  4. Save your mapping when complete

Safety Warning: Be cautious when working with electrical panels. If you're unsure, consult a professional electrician.
