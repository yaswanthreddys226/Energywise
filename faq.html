Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about energy conservation and using Energy Wise.

What is Energy Wise?

Energy Wise is a comprehensive platform designed to help households understand, track, and reduce their energy consumption. Our tools include bill analysis, energy usage simulation, personalized recommendations, and gamification features to make energy conservation engaging and rewarding.

Is Energy Wise free to use?

Yes, Energy Wise is completely free for individual households. We offer premium features for businesses and organizations that require advanced analytics and reporting capabilities.

How can Energy Wise help me save money?

Energy Wise helps you save money by:

  • Analyzing your energy bills to identify patterns and anomalies
  • Simulating different usage scenarios to find optimal energy consumption habits
  • Providing personalized recommendations based on your specific household profile
  • Tracking your progress over time and showing your savings
  • Educating you about energy-efficient practices and technologies

Users typically see a 10-20% reduction in their energy bills within the first three months of consistent use.

How do I upload my energy bill?

You can upload your energy bill by:

  1. Going to the "Bills" section in the navigation menu
  2. Clicking on the "Upload Bill" button
  3. Either dragging and dropping your bill file (PDF or image) or clicking to browse your files
  4. Confirming the upload and waiting for our system to analyze it

Our OCR technology will automatically extract relevant information from your bill, but you can also manually input data if needed.

What types of bills can Energy Wise analyze?

Energy Wise can analyze electricity, gas, and water bills from most major providers. Our system is constantly being updated to recognize new bill formats. If your bill format isn't recognized, you can still manually input the data, and we'll work on adding support for that format in future updates.

Is my bill data secure?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. All bill data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We only extract the necessary information for analysis and don't store sensitive personal information like your payment details. You can delete your bill data at any time from your account settings.

How accurate is the energy simulation?

Our energy simulation is based on real-world data and sophisticated models that account for various factors including appliance efficiency, usage patterns, seasonal variations, and local energy rates. While no simulation can be 100% accurate, our models typically achieve 85-95% accuracy when compared to actual energy consumption. The more information you provide about your household and usage patterns, the more accurate the simulation becomes.

Can I simulate solar panel installation?

Yes, our simulation tool allows you to model the impact of installing solar panels on your energy consumption and bills. You can specify the size of the system, orientation, and other parameters to get a realistic estimate of potential savings and payback period. We also provide information about available incentives and rebates in your area.

How do I create different household profiles?

To create different household profiles in the simulation:

  1. Go to the "Simulation" page
  2. Click on "Household Profiles" in the control panel
  3. Select "Create New Profile"
  4. Enter details about household size, home type, appliances, and usage patterns
  5. Save the profile with a descriptive name

You can create multiple profiles to compare different scenarios or track changes in your household over time.

How do I earn points and badges?

You can earn points and badges by:

  • Regularly uploading and analyzing your energy bills
  • Completing challenges (daily, weekly, and monthly)
  • Reducing your energy consumption compared to previous periods
  • Implementing recommended energy-saving measures
  • Participating in community events and competitions
  • Sharing your achievements on social media
  • Referring friends and family to Energy Wise

Different actions earn different point values, and accumulating points unlocks new badges and achievement levels.

Can I redeem my points for real rewards?

Yes, depending on your location, you may be able to redeem your points for various rewards including:

  • Discounts on energy-efficient appliances and products
  • Gift cards from partner retailers
  • Donations to environmental causes
  • Entries into monthly prize drawings

Visit the "Rewards" section to see what's available in your area.

How do I change my account settings?

To change your account settings:

  1. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner
  2. Select "Account Settings" from the dropdown menu
  3. Here you can update your profile information, change your password, manage notification preferences, and adjust privacy settings

Can I delete my account and data?

Yes, you have full control over your account and data. To delete your account:

  1. Go to Account Settings
  2. Scroll to the bottom and click on "Delete Account"
  3. Confirm your decision

This will permanently delete all your personal information, bill data, and usage history from our systems. Please note that this action cannot be undone.
